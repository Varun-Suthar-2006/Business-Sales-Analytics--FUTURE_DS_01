# Business-Sales-Analytics-Task1
Interactive business sales analytics dashboard with KPI analysis, revenue trends, and actionable insights
Business Sales Performance Analytics Dashboard
Project Overview
This project is a Business Sales Performance Analytics Dashboard built using Power BI.
It analyses retail sales data to uncover insights about revenue, profit, product performance, regional trends, and customer behaviour.
The goal of this project is to demonstrate how data analytics can support real business decision-making.
________________________________________
Objective
•	Analyze sales performance across different categories and regions
•	Identify top-performing products and customers
•	Understand profit trends and revenue distribution
•	Provide actionable business insights
________________________________________
Dataset
Source: Kaggle Superstore Dataset
The dataset includes:
•	Order Date
•	Sales
•	Profit
•	Quantity
•	Product Category & Sub-category
•	Region
•	Customer Information
•	Product Details
________________________________________
Tools Used
•	Power BI (Dashboard & Visualization)
•	Microsoft Excel (Data Cleaning)
________________________________________


Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
•	Total Sales
•	Total Profit
•	Total Quantity
•	Profit Margin
•	Top Category
•	Top Region
________________________________________
Dashboard Features
✔ Sales Trend Analysis (Time Series)
✔ Category-wise Performance
✔ Region-wise Sales & Profit
✔ Top Products Analysis
✔ Customer Insights
✔ Interactive Filters (Slicers)
________________________________________
Key Insights
•	Technology category generates the highest revenue
•	West region is the top-performing region in sales and profit
•	Sales show seasonal variation with peak months
•	A small number of products contribute to majority of revenue
•	Profitability varies significantly across categories
________________________________________
Business Recommendations
•	Focus on high-profit categories like Technology
•	Improve pricing strategy for low-margin products
•	Increase stock during peak demand months
•	Expand operations in high-performing regions
•	Improve customer retention for top buyers
